Lansdowne, is a cantonment town in Pauri Garhwal district in the Indian state of Uttarakhand.

Lansdowne or Lansdown is a town and a hill station situated on the Kotdwar - Pauri road at a height of 1,706 m in Uttarakhand, India. Lansdowne is located in the Pauri district of Uttarakhand, 45 km from Kotdwar.
