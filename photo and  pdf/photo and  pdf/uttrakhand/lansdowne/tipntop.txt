Tip N Top, also commonly known as Tiffin Top, is a small hilltop viewpoint that is located at 1700 metres above the sea level and provides visitors with magnificent, breathtaking views. This famous attraction is located just 1. 5 kilometres away from the main city of Lansdowne and is the centre of attraction of the entire region. The name itself suggests that the place is the highest point in all of Lansdowne, and the attraction is like heaven reincarnate on Earth.
